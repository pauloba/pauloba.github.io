Requirements

Job offers have to include the following:

  • Location, remote or on-site.
  • Name and description of the company, with a real link to their website.
  • Description of the responsabilities to be acquired in the first year.
  • A salary offer that matches a senior cloud engineer profile: www.levels.fyi
  • Additional benefits (health insurance, gym subscription, restaurant card)

Strong preferences

I would strongly prefer offers that include:

  • Remote work.
  • Clear career progression paths.
  • Respect for work-life balance: flexible working hours.
  • Economical contributions from the company side to open source projects used internally.
  • Good practices in software development: code reviews, automated tests, CI/CD and documentation.
